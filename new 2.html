MIT-WPU

MIT World Peace University (MITWPU) in Pune, India, stands out for its commitment to integrating academic excellence with the principles of peace and harmony. Established in 2017, the university offers a diverse range of programs in fields such as Engineering, Management, and Law, emphasizing experiential learning and industry relevance. With a focus on holistic development, MITWPU fosters a learning environment that blends modern educational practices with a strong ethical foundation, preparing students to become responsible global citizens. Its state-of-the-art campus and dedication to research, international collaboration, and community engagement further enhance its mission to nurture both professional and personal growth.

COEP

COEP, or College of Engineering Pune, is one of the premier engineering institutions in India. It was established in 1854 and is known for its rigorous academic programs and strong emphasis on research and innovation. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, COEP offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology fields.
