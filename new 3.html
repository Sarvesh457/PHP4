Rajgad Fort

Rajgad Fort, located about 60 kilometers southwest of Pune, Maharashtra, is one of the most significant and historically important forts in the region. The fort played a crucial role during the rise of the Maratha Empire and served as its first capital under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Torna Fort

Torna Fort, also known as Prachandagad, is a historically significant fort located about 60 kilometers southwest of Pune, Maharashtra. It is notable for its role in the early history of the Maratha Empire and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

Sinhgad Fort

Sinhagad Fort, located approximately 30 kilometers southwest of Pune, Maharashtra, is one of the most renowned and historically significant forts in the region. Perched on a hilltop, it offers both historical insights and stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Lohagad Fort

Lohagad Fort, situated about 60 kilometers northwest of Pune, Maharashtra, is a historic hill fort renowned for its robust fortifications and scenic beauty. It is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts, offering a glimpse into the region's past while providing stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
