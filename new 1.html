Welcome to Pune

Pune is one of the largest IT hubs in India. It is also one of the most important automobile and manufacturing hubs of India. Pune is often referred to as the "Oxford of the East" because of its highly regarded educational institutions. It has been ranked "the most liveable city in India" several times.
